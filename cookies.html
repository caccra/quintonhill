Cookie Policy

Quinton Hill Concierge Services Ltd  ·  Last updated: July 2026

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, which cookies our website (quintonhillconcierge.com) uses, and how you can manage them. It should be read alongside our Privacy Policy.

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They allow the site to recognise your browser on return visits, remember preferences, and understand how pages are being used. Cookies cannot run programs or deliver viruses to your computer.

Similar technologies — including local storage and session storage — may be used for the same purposes and are covered by this policy.

2. Cookies We Use

Our website is primarily informational and contact-driven. We use a limited number of cookies necessary for the site to function correctly.

Cookie / Technology Type Purpose Duration
Session state Essential Maintains your session as you navigate between pages Session (deleted when browser closes)
Form submission tokens Essential Prevents duplicate form submissions and protects the contact form Session
Google Fonts Functional Loads the typefaces used across this website (Cormorant Garant, Inter, Manrope) from Google's font delivery network 1 year (Google's cache)

We do not currently use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics platforms that profile individual visitors. If this changes, this policy will be updated at least 30 days before such cookies are introduced.

3. Essential vs Non-Essential Cookies

Essential cookies are strictly necessary for the website to work. Without them, services such as the contact form cannot function. These cannot be disabled through our site.

Non-essential cookies — such as analytics or advertising cookies — are not currently used on this site. If we introduce them in future, we will request your consent before setting them.

4. Third-Party Content

Our website loads fonts from Google Fonts. When your browser requests these fonts, Google's servers may set their own cookies. These are governed by Google's Privacy Policy. We have no control over cookies set by third-party servers.

Our website does not embed YouTube videos, Google Maps iframes, Facebook plugins, or other third-party widgets that commonly set tracking cookies.

5. How to Manage Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies may prevent the contact form and other site features from working correctly.

  • Google Chrome — Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Mozilla Firefox — Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Apple Safari — Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Microsoft Edge — Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data

For more guidance on managing cookies, visit aboutcookies.org.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy if we add new features or third-party integrations that involve cookies. Material changes will be published on this page with an updated date. We recommend checking this page periodically.
