About Us

Simplifying Business,
Travel, Relocation & Investment

Most of our clients came to Uganda — or started a business here — knowing exactly what they wanted to build. The administrative layer that came with it was the surprise. We handle that layer.

Our Mission

We simplify the process.
You seize the opportunity.

Most of our clients came to us with a problem they'd been trying to solve on their own for longer than they should have — a delayed registration, a visa rejection, a due diligence question nobody would answer straight. Uganda is genuinely full of opportunity. The paperwork around it is equally genuine.

We cover business registration, compliance, travel and immigration, executive relocation, investment verification, concierge services, and private events. Not because we set out to build a long menu — but because these are the things that sit between our clients and whatever they're actually trying to do.

We're based in Kampala. We know the city, the regulatory bodies, the right neighbourhoods, and the people who actually get things done. That kind of local depth takes years to build. It's what we bring to every engagement.

"People come to us when they've already spent too long on something that shouldn't be their problem. Our job is to make sure it never becomes their problem again."
— Quinton Hill, Founder & Managing Director

Our Values

How we
actually work

⚖️

Honest answers

If a process will take three weeks, we tell you three weeks. If your plan has a problem, we say so before you commit. You make better decisions when you have accurate information — so that's what you get.

👔

Reliable when it counts

There's a version of this service that looks polished on the surface and falls apart in execution. Professionalism, to us, means being consistent when things are inconvenient — not just when they're easy.

🔒

Discretion

What you share with us stays with us. Not as a policy — as a practice. We work with executives, investors, and organisations who operate at a level where that isn't optional. We treat it accordingly.

⚡

Gets done quietly

The best sign of a well-handled task is when the client barely noticed it happened. No drama, no chasing for updates, no explaining the same thing twice. It's done, correctly, by the deadline.

🤝

Your situation specifically

The COO of a logistics firm and the founder of a social enterprise don't have the same problems. We've worked with both. Each engagement starts from what you actually need — not what's easiest for us to provide.

⭐

We'd call us again

The real measure isn't whether a task got done — it's whether you'd hand us the next one without hesitation. That's what we're working towards in every engagement. It keeps us honest.
