A Different Kind of Assistance

Kampala concierge that actually knows Kampala

Concierge services in Africa frequently promise more than they can deliver. Quinton Hill is different: we are Uganda-based, not Africa-generalist. Our team knows which Kampala restaurants have reliable generators for dinner service, which hospitals accept international insurance on the same day, and which VIP events require months of advance access planning.

Our executive concierge operates on a monthly retainer model, giving you a dedicated point of contact who builds genuine knowledge of your preferences, schedule and priorities. For single-project concierge work — a specific event, a business trip, a property search — we also offer project-based engagement.

What We Handle

Every detail of your Uganda life

Executive PA Services

Calendar management, meeting coordination, travel booking, document preparation and stakeholder liaison. Your dedicated PA operates on Kampala time and knows your priorities before you articulate them.

Travel & Logistics

Business class and private aviation bookings for Uganda and the East Africa region. Hotel reservations at the Kampala Serena, Pearl of Africa, Speke Resort and selected lodges. Executive ground transportation with licensed drivers.

Fine Dining & Entertainment

Priority reservations at Kampala's best restaurants, including The Pearl Restaurant & Champagne Bar and the Kampala Serena dining rooms. Private dining experiences and catering for in-home entertainment.

Healthcare Concierge

Same-day specialist appointments at Nakasero Hospital and International Hospital of Kampala (IHK). Medical record management. International insurance liaison. Emergency medical coordination and evacuation arrangements.

Household Management

Domestic staff oversight, maintenance coordination, generator and utilities management, security review and property management for your Kampala residence. We liaise with landlords and service providers on your behalf.

VIP & Event Access

Access to Kampala's private events, diplomatic functions, golf club memberships (Kampala Golf Club, Entebbe Golf Club), luxury safari arrangements and exclusive experiences across Uganda and the region.

Gifts & Special Occasions

Bespoke gift sourcing and delivery, flowers, luxury food hampers, client entertainment. We manage corporate gifting for Uganda-based teams during festive seasons and business milestones.

Family & Lifestyle

Childcare recommendations, school liaison, weekend activity planning, social club introductions and family excursions to Uganda's national parks, including gorilla trekking and Murchison Falls.

Procurement & Sourcing

We source goods not available in Uganda — specialist equipment, luxury goods, specific food items — via our international procurement network. Import facilitation and customs clearance included.

Kampala Fine Dining

Where we make reservations for you

The Pearl Restaurant & Champagne Bar
Fine Dining · Pearl of Africa Hotel

Kampala's most prestigious dining address. Member of the exclusive Chaîne des Rôtisseurs international gastronomy society. Panoramic views of Lake Victoria. Champagne bar with an extensive cellar. Our first choice for executive client entertainment and investor dinners.

Reservation required 48–72 hours in advance for preferred tables.
Kampala Serena Hotels & Restaurants
Business Dining · Nakasero Hill

Kampala Serena Hotel houses multiple dining options trusted by the diplomatic community. The Rhino Bar is Kampala's most reliable setting for confidential business lunches. Consistently high standards with international-level service.

Preferred partner for post-meeting lunches and working dinners.
Mediterraneo
Italian Fine Dining · Acacia Avenue, Kololo

Kampala's leading Italian restaurant on Acacia Avenue, the city's most cosmopolitan dining strip. Excellent wine cellar and authentic Italian menu. Regular venue for the diplomatic and NGO community. Garden terrace for outdoor evening dining.

Best for informal executive entertainment in a relaxed setting.
Speke Resort Munyonyo
Lakeside Dining · Lake Victoria

East Africa's largest conference and resort complex on the shores of Lake Victoria. Multiple dining outlets including lakeside restaurants ideal for evening client entertainment. 477 rooms, 43 meeting rooms. Preferred venue for combined conference and dining experiences.

Arrange transport — 20 minutes from Kampala city centre.

How We Start

Your concierge service, from first contact

01

Discovery Call

30-minute call to understand your lifestyle, schedule and priorities. We assess which engagement model fits your needs and prepare a tailored proposal. No obligation.

02

Preferences Profile

We build a detailed profile: dietary preferences, preferred hotels and airlines, car requirements, medical information, family details, social interests and business priorities. This profile is the foundation of all our future service delivery.

03

Partner Introduction

We introduce you to our network: your preferred driver, the restaurant managers, your hospital liaison, your household maintenance team. Every introduction is personal — we accompany you on the first meeting.

04

Live Service

Requests come through your dedicated WhatsApp line, email or phone. Standard requests are handled within 2 hours. Urgent requests within 30 minutes. We are on Uganda time, operating from 7am to 10pm EAT, with emergency coverage beyond those hours.
