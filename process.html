FAQ

Common questions

How quickly can you start after I sign the agreement?
▾
Within one business day. Your dedicated concierge manager is briefed and ready almost immediately after signing, because we know that once you've decided to move forward, every day of delay costs you. For urgent situations, we can often accommodate a same-day start.
What is the minimum engagement period?
▾
Retainer engagements begin with a three-month minimum, then roll month-to-month — giving you the flexibility to scale up, down, or change direction as your business evolves. Project-based work has no minimum term whatsoever.
Can I adjust my services as my needs change?
▾
Always. We review your plan every month and adjust freely adding what you need, removing what you no longer do. Your business isn't static, and neither is our support. You're never paying for yesterday's requirements.
How do you protect my confidential business information?
▾
Every engagement starts with a comprehensive NDA. All Quinton Hill staff are bound by strict confidentiality policies, and client information is stored securely and never shared with anyone without your explicit written consent. You can tell us everything and trust that it stays with us.
Do you work with international businesses entering Uganda?
▾
Absolutely. It's one of our specialities and something we take great pride in. We handle everything from due diligence and registration to office setup and expat relocation. Foreign businesses enter Uganda with us feeling informed, protected, and genuinely ready not exposed and guessing.
